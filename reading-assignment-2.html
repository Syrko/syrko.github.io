At the Heart of It All: The Concept of Presence

The article titled "At the Heart of It All: The Concept of Presence‚Äù by Matthew Lombard and Theresa Ditton (1997) , focuses on the concept of Presence, a core concept of virtual reality which when achieved can allow for the creation of truly immersive experiences. The article examines the many different conceptualizations of Presence on account of the diverse groups of people that are interested in it, and then it attempts to draw conclusions from the body of existing research about the cause and effects of Presence.

This leads to the main conclusion that, at least at the time of writing of the article, the amount of research and data about the concept of Presence was not extensive and was mostly conjecture and theory. The article specifically highlights the need for standardized conceptual and operational definitions of the term Presence to facilitate systematic research on it, as it is an extremely important phenomenon. Systematic research should allow for the answering of many questions, including but not limited to, the actual importance of the concept of Presence and the best way to evoke it.
