Reading Assignment #3

Creating Augmented and Virtual Reality Applications: Current Practices, Challenges, and Opportunities

The article titled "Creating Augmented and Virtual Reality Applications: Current Practices, Challenges, and Opportunities‚Äù (Ashtari et al. 2020) , researches and highlights the obstacles faced by the people that develop AR and VR experiences.

Although the AR/VR field is really exciting it comes with its share of problems. As per the article, the people involved in the development of experiences come from different backgrounds, either technical or not, and face many similar barriers. Most of the problems seem to stem from the difficulty of understanding the AR/VR landscape and tools, the difficulty of finding appropriate learning materials, and a lack of guidelines, best practices and in general designing difficulties. The above problems are reinforced due to the lack of sample data and example projects, and due to the difficulties of thoroughly anticipating all user interactions and fully testing the VR/AR application.

Nevertheless, I believe that these are obstacles which continuously have their impact lessened because of the expansion of the AR/VR industry. More and cheaper equipment (available to both developers and clients), and the need for the development of more and more AR/VR experiences will lead us to the establishment of general rules, best practices and guidelines, empirically and scientifically tested, that will move us through the barriers mentioned in the article.
